THE HISTORY!

The Philippines is not just a nation; it's a story of resilience, forged through centuries of adversity and triumph. Its history is a testament to the strength of its people, who have navigated the turbulent waters of colonization, war, and natural disasters with unwavering courage. From the ancient kingdoms of the pre-colonial era to the modern struggles for democracy and equality, each chapter in the Philippines' narrative is marked by the indomitable spirit of its citizens. Today, as the country stands at the crossroads of progress and tradition, it continues to write new chapters in its saga, guided by the timeless values of unity, perseverance, and hope.

There are reliable records for four main periods of Philippine history:

  1. Spanish rule (1521–1898)
  2. American rule (1898–1946)
  3. Japanese occupation (1941–1946)
  4. Philippine self rule (1946–present)
